Privacy Policy

Easy Pickleball Scoreboard

Effective date: June 29, 2026

Summary

Easy Pickleball Scoreboard is a free scorekeeping app. The app stores game data locally on your device and uses Google AdMob to display ads.

Data collected by the app

The app does not require an account and does not transmit your pickleball scores, team names, or game history to us. To show ads, Google AdMob may receive device, advertising, usage, diagnostics, and approximate location information as described by Google. We do not sell your personal information.

Advertising

Easy Pickleball Scoreboard uses Google AdMob to serve ads. AdMob may use information from your device to deliver, measure, and improve ads. The app requests non-personalized ads by default.

Local game data

Current games and completed game history may be stored locally on your device so the score can resume after closing the app and so finished games can appear in history. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Contacting support

If you email support, we will receive the email address and information you choose to include so we can respond. Support emails are not sold or used for advertising.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

Questions can be sent to pickleballscorekeeperapp@proton.me.
